Vegetarian Pizza

Description

Make the BEST veggie pizza at home!

This mouthwatering vegetarian pizza recipe features fresh cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, artichoke, spinach and more.

Recipe yields two 11-inch pizzas or 1 large (about 6 to 8 servings).

Ingredients

  • 1 batch easy whole wheat pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 2 to 3 cups shredded low-moisture part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup jarred or canned artichoke
  • ½ cup fresh red or orange bell pepper
  • ½ cup red onion, cut into thin wedges
  • ½ cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • ½ cup sliced almonds
  • Optional garnishes: Fresh basil, red pepper flakes and/or finely grated Parmesan cheese
